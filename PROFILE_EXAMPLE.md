## Critical Fields for Job Matching:

The matching algorithm weighs these factors:

1. **Skills (25%)** - Add as many relevant skills as possible!
2. **Field of Study (20%)** - Must match job's education requirement (e.g., "Information Technology", "Computer Science")
3. **Industry (15%)** - Your desired industries must overlap with job's industry
4. **Job Title (15%)** - Your desired titles must match the job posting
5. **Experience Level (10%)** - Entry Level, Mid Level, etc.
6. **Work Arrangement (10%)** - Remote, Hybrid, On-site
7. **Job Type (5%)** - Full-time, Part-time, etc.

**Minimum Match Score:** 40% (jobs below this won't show)

## Example: Why Jobs Might Not Match

**If you see "No job matches found":**

❌ **Wrong:**
- Field of Study: "IT" (too vague)
- Skills: Only 1-2 skills added
- Desired Job Titles: None added
- Desired Industries: None added

✅ **Correct:**
- Field of Study: "Information Technology" or "Computer Science" (matches job requirements)
- Skills: 10+ skills added (JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, etc.)
- Desired Job Titles: 3-5 titles added (Software Developer, Web Developer, etc.)
- Desired Industries: 3-5 industries added (Information Technology, Software Development, etc.)
